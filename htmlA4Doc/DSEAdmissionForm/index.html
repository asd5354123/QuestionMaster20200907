Remarks :
+ 此試場設有空調 Air-conditioned examination centre.
1.
中國語文及英國語文科口試的過程會被錄影。
The proceedings of the Chinese Language and English Language speaking examinations will be video-recorded.
2.
中國語文（廣東話）及英國語文科聆聽部分將經由香港電台第二台或紅外線接收系統廣播。在使用電台廣播的試場，考生須自備耳筒式收音機應試。在使用紅外線接收系統廣播的試場（以 '#' 號顯示的試場），考生須自備耳筒應試但無須攜帶收音機。
The Chinese Language (Cantonese) and English Language listening components will be broadcast by RTHK Radio 2 or via the Infra-red (IR) Transmission System. For radio-broadcast centres, candidates must bring their own radio set equipped with earphones to take the examination. For IR centres (centres with '#'), candidates must bring their own earphones, but they need not bring a radio to the centre.
3.
中國語文（普通話）及音樂科卷一（聆聽）將以學校廣播系統播放（以'&'號顯示的試場）考生毋須自備耳筒式收音機。
The Chinese Laguage (Putonghua) and Music Paper 1 (Listening) will be broadcast via school's public address (PA) system (centres with '&'); candidates need not bring a radio to the centre.
